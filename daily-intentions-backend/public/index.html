How it works

A simple practice, beautifully supported.

1

Set your intention

Take a moment each day to reflect on how you want to show up. Write your intention with clarity and purpose.

2

See it on your home screen

Your intention appears on your home screen widget, keeping you connected to what matters throughout your day.

3

Reflect and renew

At the end of the day, reflect on your intention. Then set a new one for tomorrow, building a sustainable practice.

Your data stays with you

All your content is stored in iCloud, synced across your Apple devices. No accounts required, no data collection, no tracking. Just your intentions, where they belong.
