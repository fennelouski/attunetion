# Prompt for Building Attunetion Marketing Website

## Context
Build a minimalistic marketing website for **Attunetion**, an iOS/macOS app that helps users set and track daily, weekly, and monthly intentions. The website should be clean, honest, and match the app's design language.

**Important:** Today's date is **Tuesday, December 2, 2025**. Use this for any date references if needed.

---

## Brand & App Overview

### App Name
**Attunetion** (pronounced "at-tune-tion")

### What the App Does
- Helps users set and track intentions for each day, week, and month
- Stores all data locally on user's device, synced via iCloud
- Offers optional AI-powered suggestion features for personalized intention ideas
- Provides beautiful, customizable themes for intention cards
- Includes widget support for iOS home screen
- Available for iOS and macOS

### Core Philosophy
The app is built around the practice of **setting intentions** rather than goals. Intentions focus on the *why* and *how* you want to show up, not just outcomes. The app helps users stay focused on what matters most by creating a daily practice of mindful intention-setting.

### Key Features (Real, Don't Fabricate)
1. **Daily, Weekly, Monthly Intentions** - Set intentions across different time scopes
2. **iCloud Sync** - All content stays in iCloud, synced across Apple devices
3. **Beautiful Themes** - Three app themes (Serenity, Sunset, Ocean) with customizable intention card themes
4. **Home Screen Widget** - Quick access to current intentions
5. **Suggestion Features** - Optional AI-powered suggestions for intentions (requires user consent for data sharing)
6. **Customizable Fonts** - System default or serif/script options
7. **Reminders** - Optional notifications to help maintain practice
8. **Privacy-First** - No accounts required, data stored locally, transparent about data usage

### What NOT to Include
- ❌ Don't make up testimonials or user reviews
- ❌ Don't show fake user counts or statistics
- ❌ Don't promise features that don't exist
- ❌ Don't mention "AI" explicitly (use "suggestion features" or "personalized suggestions")
- ❌ Don't make exaggerated health/wellness claims
- ❌ Don't use out-of-date references or made-up dates

## Website Structure

### Page 1: Homepage (`/` or `index.html`)

**Hero Section:**
- Headline: "Stay focused on what matters most"
- Subheadline: "Attunetion helps you set and track daily, weekly, and monthly intentions with a simple, privacy-first app for iOS and macOS."
- CTA Button: "Download on the App Store" (can be placeholder/non-functional link)
- Hero Image: iPhone mockup showing the app (can be a simple frame with app screenshot placeholder or minimal illustration)

**Features Section:**
- Grid or cards showing key features (3-6 features)
- Each feature: icon + title + 1-2 sentence description
- Suggested features to highlight:
  - "Your Content Stays in iCloud" (privacy focus)
  - "Daily, Weekly, Monthly Intentions"
  - "Beautiful Themes"
  - "Home Screen Widget"
  - "Personalized Suggestions" (optional AI features)
  - "Apple Ecosystem"

**How It Works Section:**
- Simple 3-step process:
  1. Set your intention
  2. See it on your home screen
  3. Reflect and renew
- Minimal illustration or icon for each step

**Privacy Section:**
- Emphasize: "Your data stays with you"
- Brief explanation: All content stored in iCloud, no accounts required
- Link to Privacy Policy: `https://attunetion.vercel.app/legal/privacy-policy.html`

**Footer:**
- Links: About | Philosophy | Privacy Policy | Terms of Service | EULA
- Copyright: "© 2025 Attunetion. All rights reserved."
- App Store badge (can be placeholder)

### Page 2: Philosophy Page (`/philosophy.html`)

**Purpose:** Explain the philosophy and benefits of setting daily intentions

**Suggested Content Structure:**

**Hero/Intro:**
- Headline: "The Power of Daily Intentions"
- Brief intro: What intentions are and why they matter

**Section: Intentions vs. Goals**
- Explain the difference:
  - **Goals** are about outcomes ("I want to lose 10 pounds")
  - **Intentions** are about how you show up ("I choose to nourish my body")
- Why intentions are more sustainable and meaningful

**Section: Benefits of Daily Intentions**
- Present moment focus
- Greater self-awareness
- Reduced stress and overwhelm
- Alignment with values
- Sustainable behavior change
- Mental clarity

**Section: The Practice**
- How to set effective intentions
- Examples of daily intentions:
  - "I choose to be present in conversations"
  - "I approach challenges with curiosity"
  - "I prioritize rest and renewal"
- How Attunetion supports this practice

**Section: Weekly & Monthly Intentions**
- Explain how different time scopes work together
- Daily intentions: micro-level awareness
- Weekly intentions: focused themes
- Monthly intentions: broader direction

**CTA:**
- "Start your practice today"
- Link back to homepage or App Store

**Keep it:**
- Evidence-based where possible
- Honest and non-exaggerated
- Practical and actionable
- Warm but not preachy
- Accessible to beginners
